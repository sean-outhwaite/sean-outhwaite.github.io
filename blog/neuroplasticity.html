Fake Plastic Brains

An information hazard is a piece of information that just by knowing it, could cause harm to you.

Neuroplasticity is the opposite of that. By being aware of the concept and principles of neuroplasticity, you can use that information to learn more effectively and even subconsciously become more open-minded about your ability to learn.

Studies have shown that just by understanding that your brain has the physical capacity to adapt and improve, you can improve your motivation to learn.

Growth Mindsetting

Growth mindset is the recognition that you have the capacity to learn and grow, in comparison a fixed mindset is one that believes that your ability or intelligence is pre-determined and cannot be changed.

Having a growth mindset is important when learning, especially when confronted with obstacles or difficulties. Rather than perceiving those experiences negatively and believing that they‚Äôre insurmountable, a growth mindset allows you to reflect and learn from them, leveraging the principles of neuroplasticity you can adapt and improve your chances of overcoming them.
