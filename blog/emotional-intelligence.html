EQ vs IQ

Emotional Intelligence

08 September 2025

What is emotional intelligence

Emotional Intelligence refers to all of the "soft" skills that are used to navigate the social and emotional situations that we all face everyday.

Being emotionally intelligent means being able to effectively understand, manage, & express your emotions through practicing self awareness and empathy for others.

How is EQ different to IQ

IQ is the system we use to measure cognitive ability. It is a measure of how well someone can apply things like logic or memory, to process new information or problem solve.

EQ on the other hand is the measurement system for someones emotional intelligence. Thinks of it as an indicator for a person's ability to understand and regulate their own emotional state, as well as how they understand and respond to others.

Why is EQ important

We experience difficult emotions and stressful situations every day, having a high EQ means being able to understand those emotions, remain calm, and learn from them - giving you the tools to better respond to hardship.

Practicing emotional intelligence not only helps ourselves, but it also helps others. It can foster better understanding and interactions between people in all aspects of life - from helping you communicate with your family to dealing with conflict in the workplace.
